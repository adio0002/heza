Welcome!

We're Here to Coordinate Your Transition With Care

Life and business transitions mark the start of something new, yet they also bring logistics, deadlines, decisions, and emotional complexity. Whether you're helping aging parents downsize, planning a family move, relocating for work, decluttering a space, or managing a commercial office relocation or upgrade, Heza is here to help. You don't have to carry the weight alone; we handle the complexities so you can focus on the transition ahead.

We created this service to be your single, trusted point of coordination during times that demand clarity, compassion, and structure. Our role is simple: we manage the details so you can focus on what matters most.

From thoughtful downsizing and senior transitions, to full move coordination, vendor management, home setup, cleaning, and ongoing maintenance scheduling. Our services are designed to reduce stress, protect your time, and ensure nothing falls through the cracks.

We work with vetted, insured professionals and oversee each phase of the process; planning, scheduling, supervision, and execution. We ensure your transition into the next chapter feels smooth, supported, and complete.

Our Story

Heza Coordination began from a genuine love of helping people find calm in the middle of life’s busiest transitions.

After seeing how stressful moving can be especially when juggling multiple vendors, timelines, and emotions I wanted to create a service that brought structure, clarity, and peace to the process.

Based in Ottawa, I started Heza Coordination to offer more than just moving help it’s about thoughtful planning, clear communication, and transforming a usually overwhelming experience into a smooth, beautiful journey.

We are Heza and we are here to carry the load so you don't have to.

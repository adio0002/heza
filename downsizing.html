Downsizing

End-to-end support for downsizing. Handling the logistics, sorting, and coordination so individuals and businesses can transition smoothly.

See How It Works

Let Heza handle the heavy lifting! Whether you're downsizing a lifetime of memories or optimizing your office for efficiency, we provide the structure, expertise, and compassionate support to downsize at your own pace.

Our Approach

Steps we take

01

Consultation & Transition Planning

  • Understand the reason for the transition (retirement, health, relocation, estate)
  • Discuss transition constraints, focus points (family dynamics), and timeline
  • Define goals: what the new space looks like, what matters most
  • Create a coordinated, phased transition plan
🎯 Goal: Build trust and create a clear, personalized roadmap
02

Inventory & Space Assessment

  • Walk through the home or designated space and catalog all belongings
  • Identify items with sentimental, financial, or practical value
  • Assess what will fit in the new space
  • Flag items for family discussions, sale, donation, or disposal
🎯 Goal: Get a complete picture before any decisions are made
03

Sorting & Decision Support

  • Guide clientthrough item-by-item decisions
  • Facilitate conversations between stakeholders (familiy members, business partners) when needed
  • Honour sentimental value while staying practical
  • Document decisions clearly for transparency
🎯 Goal: Make decisions feel supported, not rushed
04

Planning the New Home or Office Space

  • Create a layout plan for the new, smaller space
  • Advise on what furniture and items will realistically fit
  • Identify what new items may be needed in the new space
  • Prioritize comfort, accessibility, and daily function
🎯 Goal: Ensure the new space feels comfortable and intentional
05

Coordinating Donations, Sales & Disposal

  • Arrange donation pickups with local charities and organizations
  • Coordinate estate sales or consignment for valuables
  • Manage junk removal and responsible disposal
  • Handle logistics so families don't have to
🎯 Goal: Ensure belongings are handled with respect and responsibility
06

New Office or Home Setup

  • Coordinate vetted movers for the transition day
  • Supervise loading, transport, and unloading
  • Oversee unpacking and placement in the new space
  • Ensure the new space is set up to feel like home from day one
🎯 Goal: A smooth, dignified arrival in the new space
07

Check-In & Follow-Up

  • Follow up after the move to ensure comfort and satisfaction
  • Address any remaining items or last-minute needs
  • Provide referrals to additional community or wellness resources if needed
🎯 Goal: Ensure the transition feels complete and supported

What You Get

A thoughtful, fully coordinated downsizing experience where every decision is made with care, every logistic is handled professionally and your new space feels comfortable and intentional from day one.

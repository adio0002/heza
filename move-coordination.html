Move Coordination

End-to-end oversight of your relocation process, from planning, selecting vetted movers to unpacking the last box so you never have to juggle logistics alone.

See How It Works

Our Move Coordination service provides end-to-end support for individuals, families and businesses during a relocation. Instead of managing movers, suppliers, scheduling, and logistics yourself, your dedicated Heza coordinator oversees the entire process; ensuring efficiency, reliability, and a stress-free experience from the first walkthrough to the final placement.

Our Approach

Steps we take

01

Initial Consultation & Needs Assessment

  • Conduct a walkthrough (virtual or in person)
  • Identify scope: size of move, timelines, special handling items, storage needs
  • Budget discussion and expectation setting
  • Create a customized moving plan
🎯 Goal: Understand your needs and define the service level required
02

Vetting & Selecting Movers and Service Providers

  • Research and evaluate moving companies based on experience, pricing, insurance, and reviews
  • Verify contractor credentials (licensing, WSIB, liability insurance)
  • Compare quotes and shortlist best-fit suppliers
  • Present curated options to the client or select on their behalf
🎯 Goal: Ensure only qualified, reliable professionals are engaged
03

Planning & Logistics Coordination

  • Create timeline and task schedule for packing, moving, and setup
  • Book movers, packing teams, storage units, or cleaning services
  • Arrange required materials (boxes, wraps, labels, equipment)
  • Prepare inventory tracking system for shipping and delivery
🎯 Goal: Align all moving components and avoid scheduling conflicts
04

Pre-Move Preparation

  • Oversee packing and organization techniques
  • Implement labeling system for efficiency and easy unpacking
  • Coordinate decluttering, donations, disposal, and item categorization
  • Communicate updates to client throughout process
🎯 Goal: Ensure the move is organized before moving day arrives
05

Moving Day Supervision

  • Coordinator is on-site or remotely supervises all operations
  • Confirm arrival of movers and service teams
  • Monitor loading/unloading, item handling, and transportation
  • Update client on move progress in real time
  • Troubleshoot issues instantly and maintain workflow
🎯 Goal: Ensure safe and efficient execution of the move
06

Transition & Post-Move Support

  • Oversee unloading and placement of items in the new location
  • Coordinate furniture setup, cleaning, and utility activation if required
  • Inventory check to ensure nothing is lost or damaged
  • Optional add-ons: unpacking, organizing, disposal of packing materials
🎯 Goal: Create a seamless transition into the new space
07

Follow-Up & Quality Assurance

  • Verify client satisfaction after move completion
  • Assist with returns, claims, or final touch services if needed
  • Collect feedback for continuous improvement
🎯 Goal: Maintain trust and deliver lasting value

What You Get

A move coordination service that streamlines the entire moving process by handling planning, scheduling, and logistics management on your behalf. With professional oversight, vetted suppliers, and hands-on supervision from start to finish. You enjoy a smooth and organized transition without the stress of coordinating it yourself.
